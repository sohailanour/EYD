What is the Technology Fellowship for Arab Youth?

+

It is a platform that will connect the Arab youth with the needed skills that will support their journey within the tech sector. This program will prepare 100 youth by providing them with a series of workshops, training programs, certified sessions, mentorship from the top technology and innovation partners.
