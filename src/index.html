about the project

Call of the Project

The “Arab Youth Technical Fellowship” program is concerned with providing young people with advanced skills to enable them economically through an integrated way that combines the theoretical and practical aspects according to the latest practices needed by the labor market to bridge the digital skills gap in various fields.

The program aims to prepare 100 young men and women from the Arab world by providing a series of sessions, workshops, training, and professional programs in partnership with technology sector partners.
